AI as an emerging technology:
While AI is nothing new, many career fields lacked the amount of data or need of dataprocessing that AI brings. In the case of agriculture, with better data collection methods that we have now, use of AI to assist in data processing, whether that be detecting visual symptoms of diseases in plants like the example project, or analyzing soil contents to determine nutrient and moisture content and advise treatments, with the increasing amount of technological applications in agriculture, AI will surely be among the ranks of other technologies.
