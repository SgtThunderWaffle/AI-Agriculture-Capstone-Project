STEP 2: Gathering Datasets
How to gather a dataset:
  • Use your own data from the problem
  • Find an existing aggregated database relevant to your problem
  • Find others with a similar problem and ask them to share data with you
  • Use an initially small sample size for proof of concept and ask for more data/gather more data after initial basic development
